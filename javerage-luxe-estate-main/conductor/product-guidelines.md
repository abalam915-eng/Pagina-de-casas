# Product Guidelines: LUXU Real Estate

## 1. Prose & Tone
- **Voice:** Professional & Elegant.
- **Copywriting:** Use sophisticated, authoritative, and formal language that reflects the premium nature of the properties being listed. Avoid overly casual slang.

## 2. UI/UX Style
- **Visual Approach:** Minimalist & Clean.
- **Design Elements:** Emphasize generous white space, simple but elegant typography, and subtle shadows. Let the high-quality property imagery take center stage without unnecessary clutter.

## 3. Color Theme
- **Strategy:** Light Mode Default.
- **Palette:** Utilize clean whites and light grays to create a fresh, open feel that allows the vibrant colors of property photos to stand out.

## 4. Responsive Design
- **Strategy:** Mobile-First.
- **Implementation:** Design and optimize the experience for mobile devices first, ensuring that complex features like advanced search and property viewing are seamless on small screens, then gracefully scale up the layout for tablet and desktop users.
